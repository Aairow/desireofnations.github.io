Our Mission

Discover the heartbeat behind Desire of Nations—a family dedicated to bringing joy, growth, and love to New York.

Designed for Family, Fun, & Purpose.

Desire of Nations is a vibrant parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) in New York. We focus on launching modern, highly engaged communities where youth, Gen Z, Millennials, and families can grow together.

Our gatherings are energetic, welcoming, and grounded deeply in Scripture. We love building strong friendships and giving back to our local communities in practical, visible ways.
